LVLY

Myra Eila Matilda Kenttä Granberg is a Swedish singer-songwriter and producer. Her debut single "Tills mitt hjärta går under" peaked at number six on the Swedish singles chart and has been streamed 11 million times. In 2019, she signed with Sony Music. On 14 February, 2020, Granberg released the single "Äru min nu" and it peaked at number four on the Swedish Heatseekers Chart.
